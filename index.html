Introduction

Barcelona is the capital of Catalonia and one of Spain's top cultural and tourist destinations. Known for its unique Modernisme architecture (Antoni Gaudí), a thriving food scene, pleasant beaches, and buzzing nightlife.

  • Founded: Roman era (Barcino)
  • Main airport: Barcelona–El Prat (BCN)
  • Currency: Euro (€)
  • Best time to visit: May–June & September

Insider tip: Buy tickets to Sagrada Família and Park Güell in advance; explore neighborhoods like El Born and Gràcia on foot.

Top Attractions

Sagrada Família

Antoni Gaudí's unfinished masterpiece — an iconic basilica combining Gothic and Art Nouveau forms.

Park Güell

Colorful mosaic terraces, whimsical buildings and great views over the city.

La Rambla & Gothic Quarter

The bustling pedestrian street and winding medieval lanes beside it.
